Mini WAR!

Shuffle and flip two cards. The first card is yours, and the second card is the computer's. The highest card wins. Then, both cards are shuffled back into the deck.

In case of a draw, the card with the highest suit wins. The suits in highest to lowest order are,

♠ > ♥ > ♦ > ♣
